Christ the Redeemer

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Built by:Heitor da Silva Costa, Engineer

Christ the Redeemer, a colossal statue of Jesus, stands atop Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro.

Colosseum

Rome, Italy

Built by:Emperor Vespasian of the Flavian dynasty

The Colosseum in Rome was built in the first century by order of the Emperor Vespasian.

Chichén Itzá

Yucatán, Mexico

Built by:Maya-Toltec civilization

This Mayan pyramid was built before Christopher Columbus for many hundred years.

Great Wall of China

Stretches across North China from east to west

Built by:Qin Dynasty, Ming dynasty

The Great Wall of China is a huge barrier that spans thousands of miles along China’s historic northern border.

Petra

Ma’an, Jordan

Built by:The Nabataean

The ancient city of Petra, Jordan, is located in a remote valley, nestled among sandstone mountains and cliffs.

Machu Picchu

Cuzco Region, Peru

Built by:Incan Empire

Machu Picchu is a lost treasure of the 15th century, a rare citadel discovered high in the Andes mountains above the Peruvian Sacred Valley.

Taj Mahal

Agra, India

Built by:Emperor Shah Jahān

A mausoleum commissioned for the wife of Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, the Taj Mahal was built between 1632 and 1648.
